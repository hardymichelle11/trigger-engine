## Decisions made
- Storage adapters over inline localStorage
- Real slope with intraday-proxy fallback
- IV rank as source-agnostic adapter
- Full MC over erf for options probability
- Multi-gate alerts over simple score threshold
- Backtest as outcome replay, not full market simulator
- WebSocket as overlay, not replacement for polling
- BQ reads via proxy, not direct browser auth
- Stack reversal backtest separate from generic put-selling backtest
- Discovery scanner as expansion mode, not replacement
- Chart context as bounded secondary layer
- Self-calibration as observation log, not auto-tuning
- See docs/DECISIONS.md for full rationale
